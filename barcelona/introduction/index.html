Air pollution, meteorological conditions and respiratory infections in Barcelona. A 14-year spattiotemporal analysis.

This collaboratory project is produced by eight students of 11th grade (first of baccaulerate), in the subject at Pompeu Fabra High School.

The objectives of this research are:

  1. To analize data of respiratory infections and find out possible interactions of air pollutants and meteorological variables in these health conditions.
  2. First thing to do is to download all data. Respiratory infection data are obtained from Information System for the Surveillance of Infections in Catalonia (SIVIC). Data included in SIVIC: date, epidemiological week, year, region code, region nae, area code, area name, primary care centre code, primary care centre name name, diagnosis, sex, age group, socioeconomic index, cases, population. Our data comes from Barcelona from 66 city primary care centres and daily data of the previous parameters from 2012 to 2025.
